Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy ad quick dish, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. this recipe is quick and easy to make. Feeling hungry? A delicious omelette is the perfect quick solution. With just a few ingredients and a few minutes of your time, .

Preparation time

  • Total: appromixately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings : cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bawl, beat the eggs very well with salt and pepper in a bowl or in a frying pan.
  2. Heat the pan: add the butter and heat until melted. Add the beaten eggs and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add fillings (optional): add some cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs etc.
  4. Fold and serve: fold the omelette in half and serve with some salt and pepper.
  5. Enjoy: Serve with some salt and pepper and enjoy
